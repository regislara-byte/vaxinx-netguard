Brand Guidelines
✓ DO
  • Use Foxlotl Guard as the primary face in marketing, splash screens, and hero sections
  • Use VAXINX Bot in technical contexts — scan animations, loading states, bot references
  • Always show mascots on dark backgrounds (#03070F or similar)
  • Maintain the hexagonal texture — it's the core visual motif
  • Keep the VAXINX shield badge visible and readable on both mascots
  • Pair mascots with Orbitron type for headings and Share Tech Mono for labels
  • Use cyan glow effects to highlight mascot appearances
✕ DON'T
  • Don't place mascots on white or light backgrounds — they're built for dark environments
  • Don't remove or obscure the VAXINX shield crest on either mascot
  • Don't scale mascots below 80px — the hex detail becomes illegible
  • Don't use both mascots at equal visual weight — Foxlotl leads, Bot supports
  • Don't use generic sans-serif fonts alongside the mascots
  • Don't recolor — the blue palette is non-negotiable brand identity
  • Don't crop the tail — it's a signature element of both characters
